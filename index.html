Bike Advisor

Bike Advisor is a website for local bike riders to get acquainted with what their city has to offer them. Everything from bike racks, to bike shops, to coffee stands can be searched. Once searched, a map populates, placing pins for any nearby locations. Selecting a pin will bring up its exact location and some background info on that place.

Rally

Welcome to Rally! This is a social media website that's meant to bring people together. Using Rally you can create events that you or a group may be hosting. Once created, your event can be seen by the world. Anyone can choose to join your event to get details about location, time, who's participating, and any other details you include about it. Rally on!
